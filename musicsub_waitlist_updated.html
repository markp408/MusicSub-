Why MusicSub?

Finding qualified music and worship coverage is still mostly word-of-mouth. MusicSub brings the process into one simple platform with profiles, alerts, and job matching.

1 post Alert multiple qualified musicians and vocal leaders at once
Local match Target talent by role, city, voice part, and travel radius
Fast fill Review profiles and secure music or worship coverage quickly

For organizations

Post a substitute need with the date, role, pay, and location. Review interested musicians, singers, or worship leaders and fill the job quickly.

For musicians

Create a profile, list your instrument, voice part, or worship role, set your travel radius, and receive alerts when matching jobs are posted.

For both sides

Less back-and-forth. Better visibility. A smarter way to connect paid music opportunities with qualified talent.

Who this is for

  • Churches needing pianists, organists, singers, soloists, or worship leaders
  • Schools needing choir, band, or orchestra substitutes
  • Theaters, arts groups, and event teams needing qualified musicians or vocal talent
  • Freelance musicians, singers, and worship leaders wanting more consistent gig opportunities
